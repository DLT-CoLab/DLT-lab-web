IOTA.Taipei

Connecting the real world with the world of DLT

There’s a new chain on the block. A blockchain without the blocks, or the chain. A system with the potential to disrupt the already disruptive world of distributed ledger technology. It’s called the Tangle Network and nakamo.to is focused on harnessing its potential to shake up the cryptoworld and revolutionise long-standing industries. Based in the crypto-capital of the world, Berlin, our team is set on exploring how the Tangle can be used to connect the real world with that of distributed ledger technology and create new markets in the process - without the inherent drawbacks of blockchain.

Tangle Network Aficionados

Our team is comprised of passionate, forward thinking, Tangle-enthusiasts, dedicated to bringing the vast potential of the Tangle Network to the world and establishing it as the industry leader for distributed ledger technology.
